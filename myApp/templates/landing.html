11 Features Built to Elevate
Every Employee.

From onboarding to advanced certification — SOP Master gives your team one unified learning hub that runs itself.

Foundation
Course Catalog + Student Dashboard
"Students always know what to take next."

A clean, organized learning space where students can see all available programs, track their progress, and instantly continue where they left off. No confusion. No overwhelm. Just clarity.

01
Core Learning
Video Lessons + Progress Tracking
"Pick up exactly where you left off—no manual tracking."

Lessons automatically remember progress. If a student stops halfway through, they return to the exact moment they left. Completion updates happen seamlessly in the background.

02
Knowledge Checks
Lesson Quizzes
"Knowledge checks that can be built fast or generated instantly."

Add quizzes to reinforce learning and ensure understanding. Create them manually, upload them, or generate them using AI. Quizzes can be optional or required before moving forward.

03
Validation
Final Exams
"Real certification-level control and integrity."

Add structured exams at the end of a course with scoring rules, attempt limits, and optional time limits. Perfect for programs that require higher standards or formal validation.

04
Motivation
Certifications + Trophy Tiers
"Motivation + proof of completion."

Students earn certificates when they complete programs and pass exams. Achievement tiers unlock as they earn more certifications, creating motivation and visible progress milestones.

05
Engagement
Favorites + "Continue Learning"
"Higher completion rates through better UX."

Students can favorite courses and instantly resume their most recent lesson. The system guides them forward instead of making them search for where they stopped.

06
Management
Admin Dashboard
"Manage content and learners without dev help."

Create courses, upload lessons, manage quizzes, view students, and monitor performance — all from a central dashboard. No technical team required for daily operations.

07
Enterprise Control
Bulk Access Management + Full Audit Trail
"Enterprise-grade control over who can access what, and why."

Grant or revoke access individually or in bulk. Every access is tracked — who granted it, when it was granted, and why — giving you full visibility and control.

08
Structured Journeys
Bundles, Cohorts & Learning Paths
"Sell packages, run groups, and guide structured journeys."

Bundle multiple programs together, create private groups, or design step-by-step learning journeys. Ideal for launches, corporate programs, or structured certifications.

09
Intelligence
Analytics Dashboard + Activity Feed
"See what's working, who's stuck, and where drop-off happens."

Track enrollments, completions, certifications, and student engagement. Quickly identify your top-performing programs and where students may need additional support.

10
AI-Powered
AI Chatbot Inside Lessons
"In-lesson support that reduces support load and increases learning speed."

Students can ask questions directly inside a lesson and get AI-powered assistance. Generate summaries, content ideas, or clarifications — without leaving the learning environment. Less support tickets. Faster mastery.
